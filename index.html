An evening for the digitally curious

27th July @ The Liskerrett Centre

Get Your Ticket

Join Cornwall's digital community for the fifth evening of curiosity and conversation. Get your hands on some cutting edge demos, hear interesting talks and join in the casual conversation with our local digital community.

With the world more interconnected than ever, it’s important to meet up and connect in the real world to celebrate our passion for all things digital. Events like this encourage cross pollination of ideas between related industries and like-minded individuals. Cornwall has an amazing spread of digital disciplines, let’s celebrate them together.

The evening has a handful of talks nestled between time for enjoying the finest Cornish beverages and locally made nibbles whilst chatting with other digitally curious people. Keep your eyes on our Twitter and Facebook accounts for the latest news and join our newsletter for regular updates.
