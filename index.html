An evening for the digitally curious

15th September at The Workbox Penzance

Come along for an evening of digital curiosity and conversation. Get your hands on some cutting edge demos, hear interesting talks and join in the casual conversation with our local digital community.

With the world more digitally connected than ever, it’s important to meet up and connect in the real world to celebrate our inner geek. Events like this encourage cross pollination of ideas between related industries and like-minded individuals. Cornwall has an amazing spread of digital disciplines, let’s celebrate them together.

The evening has four short 'TED' style talks sandwiched between time for experiencing the demos, enjoying the finest cornish beverages and locally made nibbles whilst chatting to like-minded people.

Full details of speakers and demos will be released in the coming weeks. Keep your eyes on our Twitter and Facebook accounts for the latest news and join our newsletter for regular updates.
